Welcome!

Hello and welcome to the Iris email analyzer app!

This application lets you input different parts of an email you plan to send and uses the llama-index package to analyze the contents.

Click Analyze Email up top to get started!

You can enter the email addresses you plan to send to in the To, Bcc and CC fields to analyze them for being non-company emails.

And you can analyze your email message and any attachments for suspicious or confidential content.
